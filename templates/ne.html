About Ganesh Puja

Ganesh Puja, also known as Ganesh Chaturthi, honors Lord Ganesha — the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom, prosperity, and new beginnings.

Celebrated especially in Maharashtra, Odisha, and Karnataka, this vibrant festival involves placing Ganesha idols in homes and community spaces, offering prayers, chanting mantras, and enjoying cultural performances.

The festivities end with idol immersion (Visarjan), accompanied by joyful chants of "Ganpati Bappa Morya!".

Puja Preparation Tips

  • Clean the home and puja area thoroughly before the ceremony.
  • Opt for eco-friendly Ganesh idols to protect the environment.
  • Decorate with traditional rangoli, marigolds, and lights.
  • Offer modaks, laddus, durva grass, and red flowers to Lord Ganesha.
  • Recite Ganesh aartis and mantras daily during the puja period.
